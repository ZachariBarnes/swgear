New Player Guide: Why You Need SEAs & Armor

What is SWGear?

SWGear is a build planner for Star Wars Galaxies Restoration that helps you plan your armor SEA (Skill Enhancing Attachment) loadout. SEAs are crafted items that attach to armor pieces to give you stat bonuses.

Why do I need both SEAs AND armor?

In SWG Restoration, your character's effectiveness in combat depends heavily on stacking stats from multiple sources:

  • Armor SEAs - Provide the bulk of your stats (+35 per piece × 3 stats × 12 pieces = massive bonuses)
  • Crafted Armor - Provides bonus HP (up to ~800-900 from capped resources)
  • Jewelry - Looted items with additional stat bonuses
  • Backpacks - Special items like the Kashykian Bandolier that give +15 to all stats
  • Food/Buffs - Temporary boosts from crafted foods and player buffs

Understanding Core Stats

The six core stats affect different aspects of gameplay:

  • Ranged/Melee General - Speed, Defense, and Accuracy for your combat type
  • Defense General - Defense value and Heal Efficiency
  • Toughness Boost - Health pool (+2 HP per point)
  • Endurance Boost - Action, Mind pools, and HAM regeneration
  • Opportune Chance - Accuracy, Medical Speed, and Critical Chance

Stat Thresholds

Stats have diminishing returns after certain thresholds (general guidelines - optimal targets vary by stat):

  • 250-300 - Ideal range for most stats
  • 300-350 - Diminishing returns zone
  • 350+ - Overcapped (wasted points)

How to Use This Tool

  1. Click on armor slots to add stats (or use Quick Presets)
  2. Add backpack bonuses and external buffs
  3. Watch the Stat Totals panel to stay within optimal thresholds
  4. Switch to the Crafter tab to get a junk loot shopping list
  5. Share your build using the "Copy Share Link" button
